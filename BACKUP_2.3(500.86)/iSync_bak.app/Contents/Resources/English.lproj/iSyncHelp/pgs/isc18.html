Turning off synchronization for a device
 

If you don't want to synchronize a particular device, you can temporarily turn synchronization off for that device.

Click the device icon in the iSync window so that its settings appear, then deselect the "Turn on Synchronization" checkbox (so there's no checkmark).

If you turn off synchronization for a device and later turn synchronization on again, the next time your device is present (within Bluetooth range or connected to your computer) any changes that didn't get updated on the device while synchronization was off will be made the next time you sync. Consequently, the first sync may be slower.

You can also stop syncing a specific type of information (for example, contacts) to a device, or completely turn off synchronization on your computer for any device or type of information. For more information, click "Tell me more."

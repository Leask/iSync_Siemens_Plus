Choosing what information is synchronized
 

You can choose what information is synchronized on your devices. For example, you may want to synchronize only specific calendars on your Palm OS device, or synchronize only contacts on your phone. You may want to reduce the information synchronized to speed up the sync or to limit the memory used on a device.

Select a device icon in the iSync window, then choose the information you want synchronized on that device. (If the device settings aren't visible, click the device icon again to expand the iSync window.)

Tip: To synchronize just a portion of your Address Book to your phone, create a group in Address Book that includes only the contacts you want to sync. Then in iSync, choose to sync only that group. (You can sync only contacts that have a phone number in Address Book.)

I'm having problems synchronizing my Palm OS device
 

If you're having trouble synchronizing your Palm OS device, check the following:

•

Make sure you've correctly set up your Palm OS device to work with iSync (you should see an icon for it in the iSync window). For instructions, click "Tell me more."

•

Make sure you are initiating the sync using the HotSync application on your Palm OS device or the button on the device cradle (not the Sync Devices button in the iSync window).

•

In HotSync Manager, choose HotSync > View Log to check for error information. If you see the message "iSync conduit skipped," you need to enable Palm syncing again by choosing Devices > Enable Palm OS Syncing. For instructions, click "Tell me more."

•

Make sure you're using an iSync-compatible Palm OS device. For more information, visit www.apple.com/isync.

If your information still does not sync with your Palm, you may want to "start over" by replacing the information on your Palm with the information that's on your computer. For information about resetting the information on your devices, click "Tell me more."

If you're still having trouble synchronizing your Palm OS device, visit the Apple support website at www.apple.com/support/isync.

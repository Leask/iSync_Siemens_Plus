Contacts or events don't look right on my Palm OS device
 

If you use a Palm OS device, you may notice that some calendar or contact information doesn't appear on your Palm exactly as it does in the applications you're using on your computer. For example, calendar events that are scheduled for multiple days in iCal appear only on one day on your Palm, and some portions of repeating events may not appear on the Palm.

Your Palm OS device can't display all the information iCal or Address Book contain. However, any information that doesn't appear on the Palm will still appear in iCal or Address Book. So for example, if you change an event on your Palm and then sync with your computer, your changes are synced without losing the information that's visible only in iCal.

If the problem is not that the information isn't the same, make sure the date and time are set correctly on your computer and Palm OS device, then try syncing again.

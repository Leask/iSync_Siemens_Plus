Setting up to sync your iPod
 

If you do not have iTunes 4.8 or later installed on your computer, you can use iSync to transfer the contact and calendar information on your computer to your iPod. Before you can use iSync with your iPod, you need to add your iPod to iSync by following the instructions below. You need to do this only once.

If you have iTunes 4.8 or later, you use iTunes to transfer contacts and calendars to your iPod. For more information, open iTunes (in your Applications folder), and choose Help > "iTunes and Music Store Help."

To add your iPod to iSync:

1.

Make sure you have the latest iPod software installed.

Choose Apple menu > Software Update to see if there are any iPod software updates available.

2.

Connect your iPod to your computer using the cable provided with your iPod.

3.

Open iSync.

4.

Choose Devices > Add Device, then double-click the iPod in the Add Device window.

An iPod appears in the iSync window.

For instructions on syncing with your iPod, click "Tell me more."

Resetting the information on your devices
 

If you don't want to keep the changes made on your devices during your last synchronization, you can reset the information on your devices to match the information on your computer. You may also want to reset your device information if you've had problems syncing and you want to "start over."

Resetting your devices erases the calendar and contact information on your device(s) and replaces it with the information on your computer. Any contact or calendar information on your device(s) that is not also stored in your contacts or calendar application on your computer is erased when you reset your devices. For example, you may lose any speed dial, voice dial, or custom ring information on your mobile phone.

Important: Once the reset synchronization has begun, you cannot cancel it. Clicking the Cancel Sync button in iSync (or choosing Cancel Sync from the sync menu) while the reset sync is in progress only postpones the reset until the next time you sync.

•

To replace the information on a single device with the information that's currently on your computer, select the device's icon in the iSync window, and then choose Devices > Reset "Device Name," where "device name" is the name of the selected device.

•

To replace the information on all your devices with the information that's currently on your computer, choose Devices > Reset All Devices.

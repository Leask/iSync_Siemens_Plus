Synchronization takes a long time
 

Synchronization may sometimes take longer than at other times, depending on a number of factors.

For example, synchronization may take longer the very first time you sync, after you've added a new device to iSync, or if you've turned off syncing for a particular type of information (such as your calendars) and then turned it back on.

In these cases, iSync must analyze all your information, then compare the information on all devices, which may take awhile. After that, iSync will "remember" the information it synced the last time, and synchronization will take less time.

To help speed the sync with a Palm OS device, make sure the HotSync Progress window is in front. For other tips and tricks on speeding up a sync, click "Tell me more."

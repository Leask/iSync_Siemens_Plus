Responding to messages about the number of sync changes
 

When you synchronize your computers and devices, a window may appear before any changes are made to let you confirm whether you want to proceed. The Data Change Alert window tells you how many pieces of information will be added, deleted, or changed on your computer.

You can proceed with or cancel the synchronization at this point. You can also change when the Data Change Alert appears, or prevent the window from appearing.

•

If you're sure you want to continue with the synchronization, click Proceed. If you're not sure, you can click Cancel.

•

To choose when the Data Change Alert window appears, choose iSync > Preferences, make sure the "Show Data Change Alert when" checkbox is selected, and then choose an option from the pop-up menu.

•

To prevent the Data Change Alert window from appearing, choose iSync > Preferences, then deselect the "Show Data Change Alert when" checkbox to turn off the Data Change Alert.

You may see more than one window depending on what information is being synchronized and the current settings for the Data Change Alert in iSync Preferences. When you click Proceed or Cancel, your response affects only the information described in the current Data Change Alert.
